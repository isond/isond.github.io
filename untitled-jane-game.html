Untitled Jane Game

Our main character, Ms. Jane, is trying to get a career in the male dominated tech industry. She must overcome the challenges of women in tech in order to have a successful career. The goal is to complete as many tasks as possible for boss man and get promoted to CEO. Each level has a number of tasks that Ms. Jane must complete before time runs out.

My Contribution

I worked as the Asset Artist for this project. I helped put together the characters for our game. Our professor allowed us to use any available resources for our game, and so most of the office assets were based from Pokemon assets.

Although our group had two main developers on the project, I would occassionally helped with the code when needed. This gave me an opportunity to use Unity for the first time.
