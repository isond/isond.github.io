Intro to Computer Graphics: Final Project

For my Intro to Computer Graphics class, our final project was to animate a scene of a driving car using JavaScript. The assignment required that the car be driving at two different times of day -- during the day and at night. When the car is driving in the day, the headlights of the car are turned off and the light in the center is off as well. At night, the sun, which is the brightest source of light, goes dark while the headlights and the light in the center are turned on.

Unlike our previous projects in this class, we were not given a code base to work with. This had to be coded entirely from scratch. We were only shown how the final piece should look like as reference. I had to add all the necessary shapes into the scene and place them in the correct areas. I had no trouble with doing this as previous assignments had us add shapes to a scene. The part I had trouble with the most was timing the various light sources and getting them to emit the right amount of brightness.
